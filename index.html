About

I have conducted my research in close collaboration with different partners from industry, including Dell, Infosys, and Siemens within an international research network. My research has focused on the construction and empirical evaluation of software process and models for software development. My goal is to better support the different needs for software development through the application of cutting-edge software engineering technologies. As such, I have been conducting research in the areas of Global Software Development, Agile Software Engineering, and Software Engineering Education. Recently, I have become interested in Software Engineering for healthcare. My research areas of interest are:

Global Software Development
Agile Software Engineering
Software Engineering Education
Software Engineering for Healthcare
